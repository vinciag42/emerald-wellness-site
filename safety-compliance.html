Safety & Compliance

Clear guardrails for provider-supported health intelligence.

Emerald Wellness is built to organize information, support wellness planning, and connect customers to provider review where appropriate while maintaining clear medical, privacy, and prescription boundaries.

No automatic prescriptions

Prescription decisions require qualified licensed provider review where available and legally permitted.

No medical replacement

Emerald Wellness does not diagnose, treat, cure, prevent disease, or replace emergency or ongoing medical care.

Privacy and security

Health information workflows should follow privacy, security, and applicable legal requirements.

Location limits

Services, provider availability, pharmacies, prescriptions, labs, and product categories vary by location.
