# Emerald Wellness Email + SMS Funnel Drafts

Status: Draft for Vincia approval. Do not activate live sends until reviewed.

Brand position: Emerald Wellness is a personal health intelligence platform with provider-guided wellness support, licensed prescribers/doctor review where applicable, lab-informed planning, peptide/supplement/vitamin/bioregulator organization, medication and stack checking, and a secure member command center.

Compliance guardrail used in all drafts: Emerald Wellness does not diagnose, treat, cure, or replace medical care. Messages should guide, organize, remind, and educate. Do not promise results.

## Vincia-approved funnel decisions

- Post-consultation emails use provider-review language only for members who actually completed a provider/doctor review.
- Quarterly lab reminders go to everyone who has labs or a lab-tracking path, not only Platinum or Concierge.
- Sender identity should be Emerald Wellness, including shop-related messages.
- SMS should feel luxury concierge-style while still being brief and TCPA compliant.
- Exact pricing stays on checkout/pricing pages, not inside nurture emails, so pricing remains accurate.
