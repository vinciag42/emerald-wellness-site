## 8. Klaviyo connection plan

Current site connection:

- Homepage enrollment modal submits to `/api/subscribe`.
- `/api/subscribe` sends consented email signups to Klaviyo first.
- The same endpoint stores the lead in Supabase after Klaviyo accepts it.
- Klaviyo list fallback ID in code: `XEEg3P`.
- Required Vercel secret: `KLAVIYO_PRIVATE_KEY`.

Recommended Klaviyo events to add after approval:

- `Viewed Homepage`
- `Started Enrollment`
- `Submitted Enrollment`
- `Started Checkout`
- `Completed Purchase`
- `Selected Plan`
- `Clicked Shop`
- `Viewed Specialty Modules`
- `Requested Concierge`

Recommended Klaviyo segments:

- New lead — homepage enrollment
- Trial started — $1 first 7 days
- Paid member — monthly
- Paid member — annual
- Concierge interest
- Peptide education interest
- Specialty module interest
- Shop click but no purchase

Do not send to Klaviyo:

- Lab values
- Medication list details
- Symptoms entered by a user
- Diagnoses
- Provider notes
- Any protected health information

Safe properties only:

- email
- first name
- selected membership tier
- content interest category
- signup source
- UTM source, medium, campaign, and content
- purchase plan name
- non-sensitive lifecycle status
