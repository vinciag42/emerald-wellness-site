# Emerald Wellness SEO Optimization Checklist

Use this list when reviewing or expanding Emerald Wellness SEO.

## Core Positioning

Primary SEO category:

- Personal health intelligence platform
- Wellness education platform
- Medication and supplement stack checker
- Peptide education and protocol organization
- Lab and biomarker tracking
- Wearable and symptom organization
- Specialty wellness modules

Primary positioning line:

> Emerald Wellness is a personal health intelligence and wellness education platform for organizing labs, medications, supplements, peptides, symptoms, wearables, and wellness protocols in one place.

## Competitor-Informed SEO Patterns

Top health-intelligence and wellness companies emphasize:

- Clear category ownership in the title tag
- A simple “how it works” path
- Lab, biomarker, wearable, and tracking language
- Trust, security, privacy, and support links
- Plan/pricing clarity
- Strong internal linking to product, education, app, and marketplace pages
- Social preview tags for share cards
- Schema markup for organization, app/software, FAQ, collection pages, and articles
- Sitemap coverage for high-value pages

## Compliance Guardrails

Avoid SEO claims that imply diagnosis, treatment, cure, disease prevention, or medical replacement.

Preferred wording:

- educational wellness intelligence
- personal health intelligence
- organize and track
- prepare provider-review questions
- support conversations with qualified healthcare providers

Avoid:

- AI doctor
- diagnose
- treat
- cure
- reverse disease
- medical-grade unless legally approved
- guaranteed results
