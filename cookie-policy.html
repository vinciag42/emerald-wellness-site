Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Emerald Wellness, LLC ("Emerald Wellness," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website at emeraldwellness.health and use our platform (collectively, the "Services").

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owner.

Cookies allow a website to recognize your device and remember information about your visit — such as your preferred language, login status, or settings — to make your next visit easier and the Service more useful to you.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in to your Emerald Wellness account across sessions.
  • Preferences: To remember your in-app preferences, such as tab selection and toggle states.
  • Security: To protect your account and prevent fraud or unauthorized access.
  • Analytics: To understand how our Services are used so we can improve them (anonymized data only).
  • Performance: To optimize page load times and deliver a faster experience.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Strictly Necessary Required for authentication, session management, and basic platform functionality. Cannot be disabled. Session / up to 1 year
Functional Remember your preferences and settings within the app (e.g., supplement stack data stored in localStorage). Persistent (local storage)
Analytics Aggregate, anonymized data about how pages are accessed and used. No personally identifiable information is shared. Up to 2 years
Payment Set by Stripe (our payment processor) to manage secure payment sessions and prevent fraud. Session / up to 1 year

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our Services are set by third parties that we work with:

  • Supabase — Authentication and session management. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — Payment processing and fraud prevention. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — Hosting and performance analytics. Vercel Privacy Policy

We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party advertisers. We do not use cookies for targeted advertising.

5. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, our app uses your browser's localStorage to store your supplement stack, journal entries, reminders, and other personal data locally on your device. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly sync or back up your data.

You can clear local storage at any time through your browser privacy settings.

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings menu. Note that blocking strictly necessary cookies will prevent you from logging into your account.
  • Browser extensions: Privacy extensions (such as uBlock Origin or Privacy Badger) can block third-party tracking.
  • Opt-out links: Certain analytics providers offer opt-out mechanisms through browser settings or their own tools.

If you disable cookies, some features of the Services may not function properly — in particular, you will not be able to stay logged in.

7. Cookies and Health Data

Emerald Wellness treats health-related information with the utmost care. Cookies are not used to track or profile your health conditions, supplement usage, or medical history for advertising purposes. Any health data you enter is governed by our Privacy Policy and, where applicable, our HIPAA Business Associate Agreement.

8. Do Not Track

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal when this feature is enabled in the browser settings. At this time, our Services do not respond to DNT signals because there is no industry-wide standard for how to interpret them. We will update this policy if we adopt a DNT standard in the future.

9. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our business practices. We will post the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
