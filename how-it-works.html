How It Works

From first click to ongoing wellness tracking — one clear pathway.

Emerald Wellness helps customers move from product or membership selection into intake, provider review where required, lab-informed planning, fulfillment or access, and ongoing personal health intelligence.

The Emerald pathway

STEP 01

Choose your path

Select a membership, product category, lab panel, GLP-1 support option, peptide pathway, hormone wellness review, or specialty module.

STEP 02

Complete intake

Share your goals, health history, current medications, allergies, supplements, peptides, symptoms, and relevant wellness background.

STEP 03

Provider review where required

Prescription requests, where available and legally permitted, are reviewed by qualified licensed providers. Eligibility is not automatic.

STEP 04

Labs when appropriate

Labs can help establish baselines, organize safety questions, identify trends, and support quarterly review conversations.

STEP 05

Fulfillment or access begins

Approved products, memberships, modules, or platform access move forward according to the selected path, provider determination, and applicable rules.

STEP 06

Track progress

Organize labs, medications, supplements, peptides, symptoms, wearable insights, protocol notes, and provider-review questions in one connected experience.

What is not guaranteed

For prescription pathways

Prescription decisions, where available, are made only by qualified licensed providers after review. A request may be approved, declined, delayed, require labs, require records, or require follow-up. For eligible prescription peptide pathways, products are intended to be sourced through U.S.-based licensed 503B outsourcing or compounding pharmacy partners where applicable, with quality documentation where available.
