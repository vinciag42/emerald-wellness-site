# Emerald Wellness Positioning Memory

Use this positioning when answering questions, reviewing copy, or making product decisions for Emerald Wellness.

## Core Positioning

Emerald Wellness is a **Personal Health Intelligence Platform**.

Preferred full positioning:

> Emerald Wellness is a personal health intelligence and wellness education platform designed to help people organize labs, medications, supplements, peptides, symptoms, wearables, and wellness protocols in one place.

Premium short version:

> Personal Health Intelligence for the way your body actually lives.

Compliance-safe version:

> Educational personal health intelligence designed to help people organize their wellness journey, understand patterns, and prepare better questions for qualified healthcare providers.

## What Emerald Wellness Covers

- Labs and biomarker organization
- Supplements, vitamins, peptides, and bioregulators
- Prescription and medication list organization
- Wearables and biometric trends
- Symptoms and progress tracking
- Stack and interaction education
- Protocol education and planning
- Regenesis planning
- Specialty modules
- Provider-review questions

## Guardrails

Always pair the positioning with educational, wellness, and provider-review language.

Emerald Wellness should not be described as:

- An AI doctor
- A medical diagnosis platform
- A treatment platform
- A cure platform
- A replacement for medical care
- A disease-management platform unless reviewed by qualified legal/compliance counsel

Preferred disclaimer language:

> Emerald Wellness provides educational wellness intelligence and tracking tools. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or replace medical care. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider.
