About Us

The Emerald Wellness Story

Emerald Wellness was born from nearly 30 years in the medical field and a front-row seat to the evolution of medicine, diagnostics, and patient care. Through every advancement, one truth has remained constant: people want to feel their best, understand what is happening inside their bodies, and feel confident about the questions they bring to qualified providers.

After years of caring for patients, Emerald Wellness was created for people who want a calmer, more organized way to bring prescriptions, labs, supplements, vitamins, peptides, symptoms, biometrics, wearables, and wellness protocols into one connected place.
