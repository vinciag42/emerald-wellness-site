What Is Methylation?

The master switch behind almost everything your body does

Methylation is a fundamental biochemical process that happens over a billion times per second in every cell of your body. It works by adding a tiny molecule — called a methyl group — to DNA, proteins, and other compounds, effectively switching genes on and off, detoxifying harmful substances, and regulating virtually every major system in your biology.

When methylation works well, you feel sharp, energized, emotionally balanced, and resilient. When it doesn't — due to genetics, diet, or nutrient deficiencies — the downstream effects are profound and often misdiagnosed.

Why it matters:
  • Controls gene expression & DNA repair
  • Drives detoxification of hormones & toxins
  • Produces serotonin, dopamine & melatonin
  • Regulates homocysteine (cardiovascular risk)
  • Impacts energy, mood & cognitive function
  • Linked to anxiety, depression & ADHD
  • Critical for longevity & biological aging
Up to 44% of people carry an MTHFR gene variant that impairs methylation — most never know it. The good news: you only need this test once in your lifetime. Your genes don't change. Emerald's methylation test identifies your status and generates a targeted protocol to correct it — permanently informing every recommendation we make for you going forward.
