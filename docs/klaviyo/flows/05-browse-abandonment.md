# EW7 — Browse Abandonment

**Objective:** return an engaged shopper to a viewed product. **Trigger:** Product Viewed. **Filters:** has not Started Checkout or Placed Order since flow start; can receive email. **Exclude:** suppressed, no consent, internal tests, entered in last 7 days. **Timing:** +4 hours one email; optional +2 days only after baseline approval. Subject “Still exploring?”; preview “Return to what you viewed.” Copy: “Take another look at {{ event.Name|default:'the item you viewed' }} and review its product information, ingredients, and policies. No medical outcome is promised.” CTA View Item. UTM `email/ew7_browse/viewed_item`. Exit on checkout/order/suppression. Smart Sending and frequency cap on. KPI: product return and placed-order conversion. CEO status **Not approved — draft**. QA dynamic fallback, catalog link, filters, Draft.
