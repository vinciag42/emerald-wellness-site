# EW7 — Replenishment

**Objective:** remind a customer to review a product near its expected replacement window. **Trigger:** Replenishment Due generated from approved product metadata. **Filters:** purchased item, no repurchase since, can receive channel. **Exclude:** suppression, returned/cancelled item, missing window. **Timing:** product window day; optional +7d final reminder. Subject “Time to review {{ item_name }}?”; preview “Check whether you need another.” Copy: “Based on the typical product replacement window—not personal dosing—it may be time to review {{ item_name }}. Check the label and your own supply before ordering.” CTA Review Product. UTM `email/ew7_replenishment/{{ item_id }}`. SMS only with verified consent and separate CEO approval. Exit repurchase/suppression. KPI repeat purchase; never infer adherence or dosage. CEO status **Not approved — draft**. QA metadata owner, return/order filters, Draft.
