Clinical Standards

Wellness education with clear boundaries

Emerald Wellness is designed to organize information and support better conversations with qualified professionals. It is not a medical practice and does not diagnose, prescribe, or treat disease.

📚

Evidence-aware content

We aim to distinguish established evidence from emerging research, observational findings, and educational hypotheses. Content should state meaningful limitations instead of presenting uncertainty as fact.

⚕️

Clinician-in-the-loop care

Medication changes, injectable therapies, hormone treatment, GLP-1 treatment, and other clinical decisions belong with licensed professionals who understand the individual patient.

🛡️

Safety before optimization

Interaction flags and wellness insights are prompts for professional review, not guarantees of safety. Urgent symptoms and emergencies require appropriate medical services—not an app.

🔒

Responsible claims

Named experts, testimonials, certifications, compliance status, and performance statistics should appear only when they are real, documented, and authorized for public use.

Before acting on health information

  • Discuss diagnoses, prescriptions, dosages, and treatment changes with a licensed healthcare professional.
  • Do not delay urgent or emergency care because of information presented by Emerald Wellness.
  • Review our Medical Disclaimer, Privacy Policy, and Terms of Service.
  • Contact support@emeraldwellness.health with content or safety concerns.
