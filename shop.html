SEO content blocks

What Google needs to understand.

Each shop page should include a clear H1, 150–300 words of helpful copy, internal links, FAQs, safety notes, product/category schema, and descriptive image alt text.

  • One H1 per page.
  • Unique title and meta description.
  • Open Graph and Twitter card tags.
  • FAQ and organization schema.
  • Internal links to Emerald Wellness, app, labs, and support.
Important safety wording: Emerald Wellness provides educational wellness intelligence and tracking tools. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or replace medical care. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider. Some shop options may require consultation, prescription, medical review, or eligibility screening.
